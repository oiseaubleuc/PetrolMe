Our Business Model

How we earn — and why it works for you

0%

Upfront Fees

We never charge retainer fees or upfront payments. If we do not close a deal, we do not earn. This structure ensures we only accept mandates we believe in.

0.5–2%

Commission Rate

Our intermediation fee is typically 0.5% to 2% of the transaction value, agreed in advance and transparently disclosed to both parties. On a $10M deal, that is $50K–$200K.

100%

Aligned Interests

Because we are paid on results, our interests are completely aligned with yours. We work harder, faster and smarter — because our success depends entirely on the success of our clients.

Our Process

How a mandate works

From the first conversation to the signed agreement — a clear, structured process with full transparency at every step.

01

Initial Consultation

We meet with you — buyer or seller — to understand your needs, volume requirements, quality specifications and timeline. Everything discussed is strictly confidential.

02

Mandate Agreement

We formalize our engagement with a clear mandate letter — defining scope, commission rate and terms. No obligations until both parties agree.

03

Counterpart Identification

We activate our network to identify and qualify the right counterpart — a verified buyer, seller or investor who matches your requirements precisely.

04

Introduction & Negotiation

We facilitate the introduction, manage communication between parties and support the negotiation process — always maintaining neutrality and discretion.

05

Due Diligence Support

We assist with document coordination, regulatory verification and compliance checks — ensuring both parties have the information they need to proceed with confidence.

06

Deal Closure

Once terms are agreed and the contract is signed, our commission is settled. The relationship does not end here — we continue to support both parties throughout execution.
